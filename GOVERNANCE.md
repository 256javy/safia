# Safia — Governance

**Status: stub.** This document will describe how decisions are made in Safia: the contribution ladder (contributor → reviewer → maintainer), the RFC process for non-trivial product changes, the criteria a new track must meet before it ships, and the process for amending `VISION.md` and `SUSTAINABILITY.md`.

Until then, governance is informal and single-maintainer, but **every decision is expected to be justifiable against the criteria implicit in `VISION.md` §10 (decision filter) and `SUSTAINABILITY.md` §1 (funding principles).** Writing this doc becomes priority when the second regular contributor joins the project.

See also: `CONTRIBUTING.md`, `STYLE.md`, `VISION.md`, `SUSTAINABILITY.md`.
